Published: February 22, 2024 Category: Image Optimization

Why Image Compression Matters

Image compression is crucial for website performance. Unoptimized images can significantly slow down your website, affecting user experience and SEO rankings.

Quality Level Guidelines

Here's when to use different quality levels:

  1. High Quality (80-90%): For professional photography and detailed images
  2. Medium Quality (60-70%): Perfect for most web images
  3. Low Quality (40-50%): When file size is critical

Best Practices

  • Always keep original files
  • Test compressed images before deployment
  • Use appropriate formats for different image types
  • Consider responsive image solutions
