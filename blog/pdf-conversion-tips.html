PDF Conversion Tips and Tricks

Published: February 22, 2024 Category: PDF Tools

Converting PDF to Word

Converting PDFs to Word documents while maintaining formatting can be tricky. Here are some best practices:

  • Ensure your PDF is not password protected
  • Check that text is not embedded as images
  • Maintain original page layout when possible
  • Verify formatting after conversion

Converting Word to PDF

Tips for perfect Word to PDF conversion:

  • Use standard fonts or embed them
  • Check image resolution
  • Set proper margins
  • Review document properties

Common Conversion Issues

Watch out for these common problems:

  1. Lost formatting
  2. Missing fonts
  3. Broken links
  4. Image quality degradation
